Meet the Founder

Shaik Arshad, Advocate

Shaik Arshad is a passionate legal professional and the visionary founder of "Think India Consultancy". With a deep commitment to education and youth empowerment, he launched this consultancy to provide reliable guidance to aspiring B.Tech students.

His mission is to build bridges between students and their dream colleges — offering honest advice about branch selection, college shortlisting, admission procedures, and career planning.

Drawing from his strong background in advocacy, ethics, and service to the community, Shaik Arshad ensures that the consultancy remains grounded in trust, integrity, and the well-being of every student.
